व्यापार सहायक क्या है?

Vyapaar Sahayak is a multilingual customer support chatbot, designed to seamlessly facilitate communication between sellers and buyers within existing ecosystems.

The Big Idea

Our innovative tool incorporates sentiment analysis capabilities, enabling it to identify and translate customer emotions empathetically, thereby enhancing the overall support experience.

Our Scope

Unlike traditional translation tools, our advanced chatbot ensures accurate and natural real-time translation across text-to-text, voice-to-voice, and voice-to-text interactions, eliminating language barriers and fostering efficient communication.

Customer Range

With our chatbot, sellers and buyers can engage in productive interactions with confidence, regardless of language differences, ensuring smooth and effective communication every time.
